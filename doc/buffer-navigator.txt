2. Usage                                     *buffer-navigator-usage*

---------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Commands                              *buffer-navigator-commands*

:BufferNavigatorOpen
:BufferNavigatorClose
:BufferNavigatorFocus
:BufferNavigatorToggle

---------------------------------------------------------------------
2.2 Mappings                              *buffer-navigator-mappings*

Return/o - Switch to selected buffer
s - Open selected buffer in split
v - Open selected buffer in vertical split
z - Toggle zoom (increase width of buffer navigator window to max width)
